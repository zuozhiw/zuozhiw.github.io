About Me

I am a fifth year Computer Science PhD student at UC Irvine, under the supervision of Professor Chen Li. My research focuses are on the areas of distributed big data processing and query optimization.

Research Interest

  • Distributed Data Processing
  • Data Management
  • Query Optimization
